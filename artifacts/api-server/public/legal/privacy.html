HabitPup — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

HabitPup (“we”, “our app”) helps you build habits alongside a companion experience. This policy describes what we process when you use the service.

Authentication

Sign-in and account management are handled by Clerk (clerk.com), an authentication provider. Clerk processes identifiers such as email address and related account metadata according to their own privacy policy.

Data we process

  • Account information — Provided through Clerk for sign-in.
  • Habit data — The habits you create, schedules, completions, and related timestamps.
  • Companion / rewards state — In-app progression tied to your account (for example coins, pets, care actions).
  • Device notifications — If you opt in on mobile, reminders may use platform notification services.

How we use it

To operate core app features (sync habits, dashboards, reminders, companion rewards), enforce security and abuse prevention where applicable, and meet legal obligations where required. We do not sell your personal data.

Hosting and security

Application data is stored on infrastructure you choose when self-hosting or deploying this product (for example managed PostgreSQL and a Node/API host). Traffic between the app and the API should use HTTPS; configure your deployment accordingly.

Retention and deletion

Data is retained while your account remains active unless you delete it through product features or contact support. You may delete your Clerk account according to Clerk’s tools; downstream application data deletion may require a separate operator request depending on deployment.

Contact

For privacy requests, reach the operator of this deployment via the contact on the support page.
